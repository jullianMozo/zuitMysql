File and Folder Preparation for s05 (Capstone):

1. Create an s05 folder inside your individual folder (named Individual-firstname-lastname).
2. Clone the B485-resources repository and copy the contents of the S05 folder.
3. Paste the copied contents into the s05 folder within your individual folder.
4. Open activty.sql in your text editor.
5. Launch XAMPP and start the Apache and MySQL services.
